Cookie Notice

Introduction

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device when they visit a website. They allow websites to remember user preferences, login details, and other information that enhances the browsing experience. While cookies can improve user convenience, they also raise privacy concerns, as they can track online behavior. Many websites now ask for consent before placing non-essential cookies, as part of privacy regulations like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union.

Please note that the cookie banner on this website is still under construction. As the owner of this site, I do not personally track users or their behavior. However, some third-party integrations, such as Power BI and the certificate provider for embedded content, may set cookies for tracking and analytics purposes. These cookies are managed by the third-party providers, and I encourage you to review their privacy policies for more details. Thank you for your understanding.
