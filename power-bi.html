GDPR Power BI Dashaboard - Fines

Introduction

Welcome to the GDPR Fines in Europe Dashboard, a comprehensive visual representation of enforcement actions under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This dashboard offers a detailed overview of GDPR fines imposed across Europe, empowering stakeholders to gain actionable insights into the trends and patterns of regulatory enforcement.


Key Features

  • Country-Specific Insights: Analyze fines distributed by European countries to identify the regions with the highest enforcement activity.
  • Company-Level Analysis: Examine which organizations have incurred fines and their respective industries.
  • Temporal Trends: View fines over time to understand the evolution of GDPR enforcement since its implementation in 2018.
  • Article-Type Categorization: Dive into fines based on specific GDPR articles violated, such as unlawful processing, insufficient consent, or failure to ensure data security.

Purpose

This dashboard is designed for privacy professionals, legal teams, and compliance officers to:

  1. Understand enforcement trends to prioritize compliance efforts.
  2. Benchmark organizational practices against regulatory actions.
  3. Promote a culture of accountability and adherence to data protection principles.

Conclusion

Whether you're preparing a compliance report or exploring GDPR enforcement dynamics, this dashboard serves as a valuable resource for informed decision-making.
